Welcome to Cairo

Cairo is the capital of Egypt and one of the most famous cities in the world it has a mix of history , culture ,and modern life.

Top Attractions

Egyption Museum

The Egptian Museum is located in Tahrir in downtown Cairo .It is one of the most important museums in Egypt ,containing ahuge coolection of ancient Egyptian artifacts, including treasures from King Tutankhamun. It is a must-visit place for anyone interested in Egypt's history

Cairo Tower

located on Gezira Island in The Nile River in Zamalek,Cairo Tower is one of the city's most famous landmarks . It offers a panoramic view of Cairo from its observation deck ,allwowing visitors to see Nile ,downtown Cairo and major city landmarks.Is a popular tourist attraction especialy at sunset and night.

Citadel of Saladin

The Citadel of Saladin is located in cairo on Mokattam Hill.it is one of the most famous Islamic landmarks in Egypt ,built in the 12th century. It is known for its historical importance and its amazing panoramic view of Cairo.

Khan El Khalili

located in Islamic Cairo near Al-hussein area ,Is one of the oldest and most famous markets in Egypt. It is historic bazaar filled with shops selling souvenirs,spices ,jewelry,and traditional handmade crafts.

Al-Muizz street

located in Islamic cairo near Al-Azhar and Khan El Khalili ,is one of the oldest street in Cairo its famous for its Islamic architecture,historic mosques,and beautifully preserved medieval buildings. reflects the history of old cairo.
