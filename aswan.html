Welcome to Aswan

Aswan is one of the most beautiful and peaceful cities in Egypt, known for its stunning nature and the clear Nile River. It is home to famous landmarks like Philae Temple and Abu Simbel, as well as unique Nubian culture. It is an ideal place to relax and enjoy the natural scenery and warm weather.

Top Attractions

Abu Simbel

Abu Simbel is a massive temple built by Ramses II. It is famous for the sun alignment phenomenon that happens twice a year.

Philae Temple

Philae Temple is dedicated to the goddess Isis and is located on an island in the Nile. It is known for its beautiful setting and historical importance.

Nubian Village

The Nubian Village is known for its colorful houses and unique culture. It offers a special experience of traditional Nubian life.

Kitchener’s Island

A beautiful island known for its botanical garden, filled with rare plants and trees from around the world. It is a peaceful place, perfect for relaxation and enjoying nature.

Aswan High Dam

The Aswan High Dam is one of Egypt’s greatest engineering achievements. It controls the Nile’s flow and created Lake Nasser, playing an important role in irrigation and electricity generation.

Felucca Ride

A traditional boat ride on the Nile that offers a calm and relaxing experience. It allows visitors to enjoy the scenic beauty of Aswan and its charming islands.
