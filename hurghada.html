Welcome to Hurghada

Hurghada is a popular coastal city on the Red Sea, known for its clear blue waters and beautiful coral reefs. It is a perfect destination for relaxation, diving, and enjoying marine life, making it one of the top tourist spots in Egypt.

Top Attractions

Hurghada Grand Aquarium

Location: Safaga Road, Hurghada, Red Sea, Egypt. The Hurghada Grand Aquarium is one of the largest aquariums in Egypt, featuring over 1,200 marine species. It also includes a mini zoo and a rainforest section, making it a perfect destination for families and educational visits.

Sahl Hasheesh

Location: South of Hurghada, Red Sea Coast, Egypt. Sahl Hasheesh is a luxury resort area known for its high-end hotels and peaceful atmosphere. It is popular for diving experiences, underwater ruins, and beach relaxation, making it ideal for honeymoon trips.

Senzo Mall

Location: Safaga Road, Hurghada, Egypt. Senzo Mall is the largest shopping mall in Hurghada, offering international brands, restaurants, a cinema, and entertainment areas. It is a great place for shopping and spending an enjoyable evening.

El Sakala (Downtown Hurghada)

Location: Central Hurghada, Red Sea, Egypt. El Sakala is the main downtown area of Hurghada, filled with local markets, cafes, and seafood restaurants. It is also known for its lively nightlife and cultural atmosphere.

Giftun Island

Location: Offshore Hurghada, Red Sea, Egypt. Giftun Island is one of the most famous islands in Hurghada, known for snorkeling and diving. It features crystal-clear water, colorful coral reefs, and daily boat trips, making it a protected natural paradise.
