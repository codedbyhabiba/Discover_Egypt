Welcome to Luxor

Luxor is one of the world’s greatest archaeological cities, home to famous sites like Karnak Temple and the Valley of the Kings. It was the ancient capital of Egypt and is known for its rich history and unique atmosphere.

Top Attractions

Karnak Temple

Karnak Temple is one of the largest religious complexes in the world. It is famous for its massive columns and detailed hieroglyphs that reflect the greatness of ancient Egyptian civilization

Valley of the Kings

The Valley of the Kings is a historic site that contains the tombs of ancient Egyptian pharaohs, including Tutankhamun. It showcases the beauty of ancient burial art and architecture.

Luxor Temple

Luxor Temple is located on the east bank of the Nile River. It was used for religious ceremonies and is known for its stunning view, especially at night.

Hatshepsut Temple

A unique temple built for Queen Hatshepsut, known for its impressive design carved into the cliffs and its grand terraces. It stands as one of the most remarkable architectural achievements in ancient Egypt and reflects the power of one of its greatest female rulers.

Colossi of Memnon

Two massive statues of Pharaoh Amenhotep III that have stood for thousands of years, representing the greatness of ancient Egypt. They are among the most iconic landmarks in Luxor and attract visitors with their historical significance and mysterious past.

Hot Air Balloon Ride

A breathtaking experience that lets you enjoy a panoramic view of Luxor and its monuments from the sky, especially at sunrise. It is one of the most popular activities, offering a peaceful and unforgettable way to see the beauty of the city.
