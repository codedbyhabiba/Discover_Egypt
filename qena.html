Welcome to Qena

Qena is a distinctive Egyptian city known for its rich cultural and traditional character. It is famous for the Dendera Temple, one of the best-preserved temples in Egypt. The city is also known for its authentic customs and its beautiful view of the Nile, making it a peaceful place that reflects the spirit of Upper Egypt.

Top Attractions

Dendera Temple

Dendera Temple is one of the best-preserved temples in Egypt. It is famous for its detailed carvings and astronomical ceiling.

Nile Corniche

The Nile Corniche in Qena is a peaceful place along the river, perfect for walking and enjoying the natural scenery.

Local Markets

Local markets in Qena reflect the daily life of the people. They are full of traditional goods, handmade crafts, and local products.
