Welcome to Giza

Giza is one of the most famous cities in Egypt, located on the west bank of the Nile River near Cairo. It is known worldwide for its ancient monuments, especially the Great Pyramids and the Sphinx, which attract millions of visitors every year. Giza combines rich history with modern life, making it a unique destination for tourists. Visitors can explore ancient sites, enjoy desert landscapes, and experience Egyptian culture all in one place.

Top Attractions

Pyramids of Giza

located on Giza near Cairo,the pyramids of Giza are one of the Seven wonders of the Ancient world They were built as tombs for pharaohs and are considered the most iconic symbols of ancient Egypt. Visitors can explore the pyramids ,learn about thier history ,and enjoy unique exprinces such as camel rides and panoramic desert views.

The Great Sphinx

located near the pyramids of Giza, the Great Sphinx is one of the most famous monuments in Egypt . It features the body of lion and the head of a pharaoh,symbolizing strength and wisdom. The Sphinx is a must-see attraction that adds mystery and beauty to Giza plateau.

Saqqara

located south of Giza ,Saqqara is one of Egypt's most important archaeological sites. It served as major burial ground for the ancient capital Memphis. The area is most famous for the Step pyramid of Djoser,the oldest stone pyramid in the world. Saqqara also contains many ancient tombs, decorated with beautiful carvings and hieroglyphic inscriptions , showing the early development of ancient Egyptian civilization.

Grand Egyptian Museum

located near the Giza pyramids on the edge of the Western Desert , the Grand Egyptian Museum is the largest archaeological museum in the world . It showcases thousands of ancient Egyptian artifacts, including the full collection of King Tutankhamun. The museum offers a modern interactive experience that connects visitors with Egypt's ancient history in a unique and impressive way.
